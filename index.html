Spell Me - Alexa, open spell me

SpellMe, was our first Alexa skill which we rolled out for Kids. The Skill features a small questionnaire to the Kids which on invoking asks for 5 spelling from Easy to Moderate words so that Kids of 6-8 years can spell them. Our ML framework and APIs restrics the Abusive words and Keeps the Child Policy maintained. As, we are focussed over Kids' and their all round development, we keep our Skills parallel to International Child policy.
